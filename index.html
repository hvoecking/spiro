About Spiro

Spiro is a digital art tool that visualizes a particle's chaotic orbit around a black hole. Save, load, and share your patterns using a 128-bit, BIP39 mnemonic seed using the share button in the lower right corner.

Learn More on GitHub

Note: Spiro seeds are not Bitcoin seeds. Do not use your Bitcoin seed here.
